NGC Private Registry Overview

NGC Private Registry enables organizations to host their own private container registry, providing secure, controlled access to container images within their infrastructure. Private registries enable organizations to store custom images, control access, ensure security compliance, and integrate with CI/CD pipelines. NGC Private Registry provides enterprise features including access control, image scanning, versioning, and integration with enterprise systems. Understanding private registry setup and management enables organizations to maintain control over their container images and ensure security and compliance.
