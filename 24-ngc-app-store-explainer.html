NGC Overview

NGC (NVIDIA GPU Cloud) is NVIDIA's curated container registry and model catalog, providing pre-built, optimized containers and pre-trained models for AI and HPC workloads. NGC offers a comprehensive collection of containers for popular frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow, pre-trained models for various use cases, and Helm charts for Kubernetes deployments. All content is tested, optimized, and maintained by NVIDIA, ensuring reliability and performance.

NGC Collections

NGC organizes content into collections, which are curated categories that group related containers, models, and resources. Collections help users quickly find relevant content for their specific use cases, whether they're working with PyTorch, TensorFlow, RAPIDS, HPC applications, or inference workloads.
